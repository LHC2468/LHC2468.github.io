Welcome

Leo holds a BS in Mechanical Engineering from UCLA and is currently a Master's student at Stanford University with concentration in Fluid Mechanics & Heat Transfer. He is a member of the NanoHeat Lab under PI Prof. Ken Goodson and Prof. Mehdi Asheghi. His research involves single- and two-phase thermal management of high-power-density electronics. Experiences include mechanical design, thermal & fluids systems design and validation, CAE, and manufacturing. Planned MSME graduation in March 2026.

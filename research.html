Coating Impact of Pool Boiling Performance and Reliablity

Investigating the coating impact on pool boiling performance through corrosion inhibition and surface treatment. Coatings studied include gold, copper, and parylene C. Boiling surfaces are microporous structures fabricated through electroplating on silicon substrates. Reliability tests include long-term boiling stability and thermal cycling tests.
